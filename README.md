## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long do kratom capsules take to work?

**Kratom capsules** typically begin producing effects 30-45 minutes after consumption, which is longer than powder forms due to the time required for capsule dissolution in stomach acid. Taking capsules on an empty stomach may reduce onset time, while consuming them with food may delay effects but potentially extend duration.

### How many kratom capsules should a beginner take?

Beginners should start with 1-2 kratom capsules (approximately 0.5-1.5 grams total) to assess individual tolerance. This conservative approach allows new users to gauge their sensitivity before gradually increasing dosage if needed. Wait at least 45-60 minutes before taking additional capsules to allow full effects to develop.

### Do kratom capsules expire or lose potency over time?

Properly stored **kratom capsules** maintain their potency for 1-2 years when kept in cool, dry conditions away from direct sunlight. The capsule shell provides additional protection against moisture and air exposure compared to loose powder. However, potency may gradually decline over time, particularly if storage conditions are suboptimal.

### Can kratom capsules be taken with other supplements?

**Kratom capsules** can generally be taken alongside most dietary supplements, but users should exercise caution with substances that affect liver metabolism or have sedating properties. Consulting with a healthcare provider before combining kratom with other supplements or medications is recommended, especially for individuals with existing health conditions.

### Are vegetarian kratom capsules different from gelatin capsules in terms of effects?

The capsule shell material (vegetarian vs. gelatin) does not significantly affect the kratom's effects once dissolved. Vegetarian capsules may dissolve slightly slower than gelatin capsules, potentially adding 5-10 minutes to onset time. The choice between shell types is primarily based on dietary preferences rather than effectiveness differences.

### How should kratom capsules be stored for maximum freshness?

Store **kratom capsules** in a cool, dry place below 70°F, away from direct sunlight and moisture sources. Original manufacturer containers often provide optimal protection, but airtight supplement bottles can also work well. Avoid storing capsules in bathrooms, kitchens, or other areas with temperature fluctuations or high humidity.

### What's the difference between kratom capsule sizes?

Kratom capsules commonly come in sizes "00" and "0," with size "00" being larger and holding approximately 0.7-1.0 grams of powder, while size "0" contains about 0.5-0.7 grams. The size affects the number of capsules needed to reach desired dosages but doesn't impact the kratom's quality or effects. Larger capsules mean fewer pills to swallow but may be more difficult for some people to consume.
